Open the portal:-
Open the Present via Pixel website from any browser.

Click on Login:-
Use the main “Login” button on the home page to go to the login screen.

Select your role:-
On the login page, choose your role as Student, Teacher or Admin and enter your username and password.

For first‑time users:-
If you are a new user and do not have a login, contact the system administrator to create your account
and register your face.

Student face registration:-
During registration, stand in front of the camera as instructed so that the system can capture your
face and save your face encoding.

Marking attendance:-
For daily attendance, log in as a student and stand in front of the camera. Wait until your name
appears or a success message is shown.

Location / area radius:-
Make sure you are inside the allowed college area. If you are outside the campus radius, the system
will not allow you to mark attendance.

Checking your status:-
After attendance is marked, you can view your status (On‑Time or Late) and the time in your student dashboard.

Forgot password:-
If you forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page, enter your username
and token number, and follow the steps to reset it.

Getting support:-
If the camera does not detect your face, your name is wrong, or attendance is not appearing,
inform your teacher or the admin so they can check your registration and system logs.
